Our story

The Flying SCOT took off in the heart of Canterbury, blending traditional Scottish recipes with bold street food innovation. Born from a passion for honest, soulful cooking, our food only uses homemade haggis in our dishes, bringing haggis inspired burgers, lorne sausage, tatties scones and many more soulful creations to the streets of Christchurch and Canterbury.

Every dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients, sustainable produce, and a whole lot of Highland spirit. Soar above ordinary fast food experience the true taste of modern Scotland!

We offer a range of food from Morning Rolls filled with Haggis, Black Pudding, Lorne Sausage, Tattie Scones, Eggs and Bacon!! to Gourmet Pies, Scottish Tablet and more!! Come check us out to see what's on offer!!
