Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At The Flying SCOT, we are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020. This statement outlines how we handle personal details collected through this website.

1. Information we collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide via our online forms. This includes your name and email address when you send an enquiry or subscribe to our newsletter.

2. How we use your data

Your details are used exclusively to respond to your event or catering requests, and to send you updates about our food truck's operations, weekly locations, and menu specials across Canterbury.

3. Data storage & security

Your information is never stored in a database and is not shared with or sold to third-party marketing companies. Form entries are routed as an encrypted transmission directly to our business mailbox via our transactional email provider.

4. Your rights

You have the right to request a copy of any details we hold about you, or to ask us to remove your email address from our communication lists entirely. For any privacy requests, please contact Leisa or Hannah Sinclair at admin@theflyingscot.co.nz.
