Our Story

The Flying SCOT took off in the heart of Canterbury, blending traditional Scottish recipes with bold street food innovation. Born from a passion for honest, soulful cooking, we use only homemade haggis in our dishes - bringing haggis-inspired burgers, lorne sausage, tattie scones and many more soulful creations to the streets of Christchurch and Canterbury.

Every dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients, sustainable produce, and a whole lot of Highland spirit. Soar above ordinary fast food - experience the true taste of modern Scotland.

From morning rolls stacked with haggis, black pudding, lorne sausage, tattie scones, eggs and bacon, to gourmet pies, Scottish tablet and more - come find us and see what's on offer!
