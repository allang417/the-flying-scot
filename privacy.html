Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At The Flying SCOT, we are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020. This statement outlines how we handle personal details collected through this website.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us via our online forms. This includes your name and email address when you send an inquiry or subscribe to our newsletter updates.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your details are used exclusively to respond directly to your event or catering requests, and to send you updates regarding our food truck's operations, weekly locations, and menu specials across Canterbury.

3. Data Storage & Security

We operate a serverless framework. Your information is never stored in a database and is not shared with or sold to third-party marketing companies. Form entries are instantly securely routed as an encrypted transmission directly to our business mailbox via our transactional email provider.

4. Your Rights

You have the absolute right to request a copy of any details we hold that connect to you, or ask us to remove your email address from our communication lists entirely. For any privacy requests, please contact Leisa and Hannah Sinclair at admin@theflyingscot.co.nz.
